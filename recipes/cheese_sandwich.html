Cheese sandwich

Description

Cheese sandwich! Get ready to give your tastebuds some lavish spoiling from this bread and cheese wonder.

It takes less than 5 minutes to make and you can tell it's tasty and yummy.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese (Relax! Cheese slices aren't as big as bread slices)
  • Butter
  • 2 eggs
  • Chilli powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Unleash the content in the eggs into a small bowl. Scramble.
  2. Add as much pepper and as much salt as you want. I would recommend just sprinkling.
  3. Put 2 slices of cheese inside 2 slices of bread, spread the egg in between the bread and in between the cheese.
  4. Repeat step 3 above for the other 2 slices of bread and cheese.
  5. Turn on your stove (low heat), put your frying pan on it, and put one teaspoon of butter in the pan.
  6. Once the butter melts, place the 2 pairs gotten from 3 and 4 into the pan. Cover and leave for 1 minute.
  7. Open the pan and flip it's contents for the other side to brown. Leave for 40 seconds.
  8. Serve this wonder in a dish and and extract that juice you've been hiding in the fridge to wash it down.
  9. Don't worry about thanking me. I know you're grateful.
