Sun secret

Description

Sun secret! This is a secret meal that has been preserved by sages for centuries

It's a code name for garri and ice-cold water, mixed with ingredients that have long been hidden in plain sight.

It's a combination that will relax all your melting nerves.

Ingredients

  • Garri
  • Milk (full cream & liquid)
  • honey (or sugar if that's what you prefer)
  • Ice-cold water
  • Groundnut

Steps

  1. Pour garri into a bowl
  2. Pour a generous amount of your ice-cold water on it
  3. Add some honey to the mixture and stir
  4. Pour your milk generously all over the surface of your creation
  5. Add the groundnut
  6. Now you can eat your meal and relax divinely.
